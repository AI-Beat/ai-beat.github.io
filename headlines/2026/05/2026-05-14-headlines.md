# AI Headlines — 2026-05-14

- [SenseNova-U1: Unifying Multimodal Understanding and Generation with NEO-unify Architecture](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12500) — SenseTime fully open-sources a unified multimodal model that removes both the visual encoder and VAE entirely, replacing them with a convolutional patch encoder and a direct pixel-space MLP head; a two-stream Mixture of Transformers backbone processes understanding and generation jointly, with text attending causally and image tokens bidirectionally; achieves 0.91 on GenEval and leads open-source models on interleaved text-image generation. *(May 12, 2026)*

- [Useful Memories Become Faulty When Continuously Updated by LLMs](https://dylanzsz.github.io/faulty-memory/) — Continuous memory consolidation loops can degrade agentic performance below the no-memory baseline: after streaming 19 previously-solved ARC-AGI solutions through GPT-5.4's consolidation loop, the agent fails on 54% of those same problems; an episodic-only agent that retains raw rollouts without forced abstraction matches or beats every consolidator across five benchmarks. *(May 14, 2026)*

- [Predictive Maps of Multi-Agent Reasoning: A Successor-Representation Spectrum for LLM Communication Topologies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11453) — Derives closed-form spectral diagnostics (spectral radius, spectral gap, condition number) from the successor representation to predict multi-agent LLM topology failure modes before running inference; perfect correlation (1.0) between condition number and perturbation robustness, perfect inverse correlation (−1.0) between spectral radius and error accumulation across 100 trials. *(May 12, 2026)*

- [Achieving Gold-Medal-Level Olympiad Reasoning via Simple and Unified Scaling](https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/2026-05-14) — A model trained via a reverse-perplexity curriculum on 100K+ token trajectories followed by two-stage RL achieves gold-medal performance on IMO and IPhO problem sets. *(May 14, 2026)*
