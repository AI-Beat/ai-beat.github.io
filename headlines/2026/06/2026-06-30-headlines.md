- [Qwen 3.6 27B Is the Sweet Spot for Local Development](https://quesma.com/blog/qwen-36-is-awesome/) — Developer account of running Qwen3.6-27B on a MacBook M5 Max (18–32 tok/s with MTP), arguing it is now general-purpose enough to replace cloud API calls for day-to-day coding and writing tasks. *(June 30, 2026 post; model released April 2026)*
