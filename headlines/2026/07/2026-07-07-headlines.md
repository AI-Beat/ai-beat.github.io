# AI Headlines — 2026-07-07

- [A Global Workspace in Language Models](https://transformer-circuits.pub/2026/workspace/index.html) — Anthropic's interpretability team identifies "J-space" inside Claude: a small set of internal representations (≤25 active concepts, <10% of activation variance) that is verifiable via verbal report, steerable by instruction, used for multi-step internal reasoning, and whose ablation surfaces otherwise-concealed model behaviors; paper includes open-source J-lens tooling. *(July 6, 2026)*

- [Ternlight – 7 MB embedding model that runs in browser (WASM)](https://ternlight-demo.vercel.app/) — Ternary-weight (BitLinear) sentence embedding model shipped as a single npm package with engine, weights, and tokenizer bundled into 7MB WASM; produces 384-dim vectors at ~5ms/query on CPU, no API or GPU required; mini variant is 5MB. *(July 7, 2026)*

- [GLM 5.2 and the Coming AI Margin Collapse](https://martinalderson.com/posts/the-upcoming-ai-margin-collapse-part-1-glm-5-2/) — Martin Alderson argues GLM 5.2 is the first open-weight model that genuinely competes with Opus/GPT at ~15-20% of frontier pricing, threatening the 90% gross margins that currently sustain frontier labs. *(July 6, 2026)*
